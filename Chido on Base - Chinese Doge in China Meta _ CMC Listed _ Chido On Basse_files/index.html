The Chido Story

We are a community takeover project deployed on the base chain. The project was launched in June 2024, when the Chinese version was in full swing. However, shortly after launch, the developer decided to withdraw from the project and sell the bulk to the community.

Since then, the project has been embraced by the community and is being rebuilt to become a trusted Dogecoin on the underlying chain, with lower risk than projects run by traditional developers. CTOs run different projects than developers. Every community member and token holder effectively becomes a developer, with the power to drive the project forward in any way they can.

The CTO has a long-term vision that is not affected by short-term price movements. Instead, the focus is on making small improvements every day that strengthen the foundation of the project and make it a solid investment proposition.
