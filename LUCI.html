ABOUT THE ARTIST

"I’m not a developer or a crypto influencer. I’m just someone who’s been passionate about art ever since I could hold a pencil. I’ve been drawing all my life, inspired by Japanese animation, manga, and European legends and myths. At just 6 years old, I knew I wanted to pursue this passion for the rest of my life.
‍
I studied art and animation for five years and worked at three different animation studios. However, I quickly realized that despite the hard work, the pay was low, and the projects didn’t align with my personal goals. To survive as an artist, I knew I needed to take risks and embrace the exciting potential of creating my own projects—through memecoins.
‍
Before creating Luci, I worked for other web3 projects. That taught me a lot, introduced me to new people, and helped me understand this space more. Eventually, I designed the little demon you know today. I set out to create the best character and narrative possible—not as a developer, but as an artist.

Luci's community grew quickly, and I was blown away by the positive feedback from all of you. Now, my goal is to elevate Luci, not just as a coin but as a strong IP, pushing the boundaries of what we can achieve together.
‍
Thank you all so much for the incredible support and love. Luci is here, and it feels like nothing can stop the little demon.
‍
It’s Demon Time, now more than ever."
