Who is Oscar?

The first, and the father of all Shiba Inu memes which became the inspiration for Ryoshi's SHIB ecosystem.

wow study pls

The story begins with Ryoshi,

who saw the 2011 YouTube video of Oscar, the Shiba Inu dog on a trampoline, and used a screenshot from it when he introduced the Shibtoken to the world in the well-known Medium article titled
‍"All Hail The Shiba!" (August 2nd, 2020).

Medium Article →YouTube Video →
Oscar appears in this article,

with Ryoshi's comment:
‍"Next we tell some more frens and WOW MOON LAMBO PUMPPPPPPY."
Many see this as Ryoshi's prophecy, foretelling that $Oscar would one day come into existence.

Ryoshi's Archives →
Adopted and guided by the Shiba community,

$OSCAR also has the support of Paradox Designer part of the founding team of Shibtoken, who has been with Ryoshi from day one. ParadoxDesigner played a key role in the Shibtoken initial development, including the creation of the Shibtoken v1 website and the front-end design of ShibaSwap. Additionally, ParadoxDesigner is the artist behind the iconic logos of $SHIB and $LEASH, now recognized worldwide.

No one was closer to Ryoshi than Paradox,

since he was amongst the first members of Ryoshi's Telegram and Discord groups.

The Oscar meme gained global fame in 2011,

even before the Kabosu meme (#Dogecoin), which would go on to achieve worldwide recognition only in 2013.

How to buy

(psst.... boing)

$OSCAR is found on Uniswap and a list of growing CEXs. Oscar is a decentralized token and, as such, we always incentivise the use of DEXs. If you choose to utilize a CEX instead, remember to research it first to ensure it is both safe and secure.

1.

Create a wallet

Download Metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io. that will allow you to buy, sell, send and receive $OSCAR

2.

Get some ETH (Ethereum)

You can buy Ethereum (ETH) directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask wallet from exchanges like Binance, Coinbase, ByBit, OKX, HTX, etc. Make sure to use the ERC-20 network when transferring ETH.

3.

Go to Uniswap

Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app and proceed to connect your wallet.

4.

Swap ETH for $OSCAR

Select ETH in the "from" field and choose $OSCAR in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
